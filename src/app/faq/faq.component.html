Open Hackathon Competition

(Open for all Engineering/ Non-Engineering students)

Rules and Regulations

  1. This will be a 2-day event named “Oriental Hackathon- Powered by Cybrom Technology”.
  2. Maximum 100 teams can participate in the event.
  3. 1 Team can have a maximum of 4 students + 1 Mentor (Faculty Member)
  4. There will be Prize Money for the winner of INR 51,000/-& for the Runner-up INR 21000/-
  5. Scholarship worth up to 1,00,000.
  6. “Certificate of Participation” for all the participants.
  7. The Registration fees for the participating teams will be INR 500/-per team
  8. Accommodation to outstation teams will be on nominal charges.

 The themes for the Hackathon are as follows

  1. Smart Mechanization
  2. Agronomics, Meals Tech & Country Region Development
  3. Globetrotting
  4. Health & Games
  5. Cyber Safety

 Event Flow

Day-1 (Round 1)

  1. Problem Statement distribution. (Problem Statement will be provided)
  2. PPT presentation by the Teams on the basis of selected problem statement.
  3. Out of which 20 finalist teams will be chosen. (Names will be announced in Round 2)
Round 2
  1. 20 Finalists’ name announcement.
  2. On Spot Work Flow Creation & Submission by the selected teams.
Day-2 (Final Round)
  1. Event Length - 8 hours
  2. Mentor - 01 mentor will be allowed (Faculty member)
  3. Rounds - 02
  4. Round 1 will be of two hours (Basic module)
  5. Round 2 candidates scrutinized in round one will be participating here it will be of six hours (Problem Statement)
On the basis of round 2 performances, the winners will be decided. Note: Lunch is to be taken on the competition table only.
