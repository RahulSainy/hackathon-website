Frequently Asked Questions

Open Hackathon Competition

Oriental Hackathon 2k23 is open to all Engineering and Non-Engineering students across India. Whether you are pursuing a degree in technology, science, or any other discipline, you are welcome to participate and showcase your innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

The themes for the Hackathon are as follows

The hackathon will focus on the following themes, allowing participants to innovate and create solutions in diverse domains:

  1. Smart Mechanization
  2. Smart Agriculture
  3. Smart AI
  4. Smart IOT
  5. Smart App
 Registration and Eligibility

To register for Oriental Hackathon 2k23, visit our official website and click on the "Apply Now" button. Fill out the registration form with your team's details, including the names of the team members and the faculty mentor. There is no registration fee to participate in the hackathon. Eligibility: The hackathon is open to students from all Indian colleges and universities. Both undergraduate and postgraduate students can form teams and participate. Students from diverse educational backgrounds, including engineering, technology, science, management, and others, are encouraged to take part in this exciting innovation event. Note: The event will follow a first-come-first-serve basis, and registration will close once we reach the maximum capacity of 100 participating teams. So, we recommend registering early to secure your spot.

Team Composition

How many members can participate in a team?

Each team can have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 5 participants. Additionally, each team must have one faculty member as a mentor to guide and support the students during the hackathon.

What to Bring

What should participants bring to the hackathon?

Participants are required to bring their laptops, chargers, and any other necessary equipment for coding and development. It's also advisable to carry personal items like water bottles, notepads, and writing materials to take notes and sketch out ideas.

Internet and Connectivity

Will the hackathon provide internet and Wi-Fi facilities?

Yes, we will provide high-speed internet and Wi-Fi connectivity to all participants during the hackathon. You can seamlessly access online resources, research materials, and other necessary tools to develop your projects.

Intellectual Property Rights

Who will own the intellectual property rights to the projects developed during the hackathon?

All intellectual property rights for the projects developed during the hackathon will belong to the respective teams. However, we encourage participants to openly share their innovations and ideas with the community and make them accessible for the greater good.

Accommodation for Outstation Teams

How can outstation teams avail accommodation facilities during the hackathon?

Outstation teams can avail

accommodation facilities arranged by the organizing committee at nominal charges. To ensure a hassle-free stay, kindly contact our team at contact us to make the necessary arrangements.
