About Us

Oriental Group

is a center of opportunity for students, academicians, and researchers who want to build a successful career. Our directors enjoy the highest salary packages in Central India, and our faculty members are paid above the AICTE full scale, with HRA/Accommodation facilities, LTC, local transport, and assured promotion policy with a number of performance-linked incentives.

Oriental Group of Institutes was started by the founder Chairman Dr. KL Thakral in 1995. We are a self-financed premier educational group imparting quality education in the disciplines of Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, and Advanced Computer Studies. We have colleges in Bhopal, Indore, and Jabalpur, and we have a significant presence in the Central region. We also have a private university, Oriental University, in Indore.
